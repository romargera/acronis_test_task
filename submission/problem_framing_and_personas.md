# Problem Framing & Personas

## Dashboard 1: Operations Center
- Who it is for: Tier-1/Tier-2 MSP technicians and IT admins handling many tenants in parallel.
- Top decisions enabled:
  1. Which tenants need triage first this morning.
  2. Whether protection posture is drifting over 7/30/90-day windows.
  3. Which issue should be escalated with context to Tier-2.
- Why it matters: Multi-tenant teams lose time in swivel-chair workflows across disconnected consoles; this dashboard compresses time-to-awareness and improves triage quality.

## Dashboard 2: Business Growth Intelligence
- Who it is for: MSP owner, practice lead, or service delivery manager responsible for MRR growth and retention.
- Top decisions enabled:
  1. Which clients are likely to churn and where to intervene first.
  2. Where the next upsell revenue can come from based on coverage gaps.
  3. Where margin leaks exist compared to peer benchmarks.
- Why it matters: MSP owners often see technical status but not commercial risk early enough; this dashboard links service signals to business outcomes.

## Shared Drill-Down: Client 360
- Who it is for: Both personas when they need customer-specific context.
- Top decisions enabled:
  1. Which action to take now (ops hardening, retention outreach, commercial proposal).
  2. What to include in the next QBR with a forward-looking plan.
- Why it matters: Both operational and commercial workflows converge at tenant level, so one shared detail surface prevents context loss.
